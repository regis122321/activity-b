Code of Ethics of the Filipino Computing and Information Technology Professional

For the purpose of this Code, the following term are defined as follows:

Information Technology

The preperation, collection, creation, transport, retrieval, storage, access, presentation and transformation of electronic information in all its form including, but not limited to, voice, graphic, text, video, data and image.

Information Technology Professional

One who develops or provides information technology products and/or services to the public.

PREAMBLE

I will use my special knowledge and skills for the benefit of the public. I will serve employers and clients with the integrity, subject to an overriding responsibility to the public interest, and I will strive to enhance the competence and prestige of the professional. By these, I mean:

  • I will promote public knowledge, understanding and appreciation of information technology;
  • I will consider the general welfare and public good in the performance of my work;
  • I will advertise goods and professional services in clear and truthful manner;
  • I will comply and strictly abide by the intellectual property law, patent laws and other related laws in respect of information technology;
  • I will accept full responsibilities for the work undertaken and will utilize my skill with competence and professionalism;
  • I will make truthful statement on my areas of competence as well as the capabilities and qualities of ptoduct and services;
  • I will not disclose or use any confidential information obtained in the course of professional duties without the consent of the parties concerned, except when required by law;
  • I will try to attain highest quality in both the product and services I offer;
  • I will not knowingly participate in the development of Information Technology System the will promote the commision of fraud and other unlawful acts;
  • I will upload and improve the IT professional standards through continuing professional development in order to enchance the IT profession.
